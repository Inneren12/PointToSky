PointToSky helps you learn the night sky the easy way: point and know.

• Aim & Identify — Raise your wrist and get instant object info on Wear OS.
• Tonight — Tile and card with the best targets for your location and time.
• Details — Quick facts for stars, planets and constellations (type, magnitude, etc.).
• Map — Clean star map with official IAU boundaries and search.
• Phone companion — Optional map/AR views on the paired phone.
• Works offline — No sign-up, no internet required for core features.
• Privacy-friendly — No accounts. No cloud profiles.
• Open catalogs — Based on established astronomical data sources.
• Local sync — Fast phone↔watch sync via Data Layer.
• Dark-friendly UI — Designed for night use.
• Languages — English, Russian.

Notes & accuracy
Sensor accuracy depends on calibration and environment (compass, gyro, time & location). For best results, calibrate your device and allow approximate location.

Disclaimers
Not for navigation or safety-critical use. Astronomy data may contain small deviations. This app is for education and stargazing only.
